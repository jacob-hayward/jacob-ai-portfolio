# jacob-ai-portfolio

A public build journal documenting the design, construction, and 
failure of a real AI portfolio project — built end-to-end by Jacob 
Hayward.

This isn't a polished finished product dropped on GitHub. 
It's the whole messy journey, documented as it happens.

---

## What This Project Is

A personal website built around three AI agents:

**1. Resume Agent**
Ask it anything about me — my background, skills, experience, 
and where I'm heading.

**2. Visa Information Agent**
A public-facing agent that helps people navigate visa requirements 
across EU and Oceania countries. Pulls from official government 
sources on a scheduled pipeline to stay reasonably current. 
Designed to be honest about what it doesn't know.

**3. Architecture Agent**
Explains how the site and its systems were actually built. 
For the technical people who want to go deeper.

---

## Why I'm Building This

I'm an AI Automation Specialist based in Auckland, New Zealand, 
working toward a career in Europe. I wanted a portfolio that does 
something — not just describes what I've done.

The build journal is as important as the build itself. 
Hiring managers don't usually get to see how someone thinks 
through a problem, makes decisions, hits walls, and recovers. 
This repo is that.

---

## The Philosophy Behind It

Most AI systems are built by people who understand the technology. 
This one is built by someone who also spent years on the other 
side of the conversation — in sales, property, customer support, 
and hospitality.

Users and customers are always at the heart of any build — 
because at the end of the day, they're the ones using them!

So, the goal isn't to impress with any complexity. It's to build 
something a real person genuinely enjoys using.

## Tech Stack

- **n8n** — workflow automation and agent orchestration
- **Typebot** — conversational UI
- **Hetzner** — hosting
- **RAG pipeline** — scheduled scraping into a vector store
- More decisions documented in the journal as they're made
